# Role Manual: Team Manager

## Role Overview
The **Team Manager** focuses on the day-to-day operations of a specific group of members. You ensure that tasks are understood and that communication flows smoothly within the team.

## Permissions
- Manage assigned Team Members.
- Create and manage Project Groups.
- Share and organize team files.
- Monitor task progress and generate internal team reports.

## Key Workflows

### 1. Group Coordination
- Create specific chat groups for different work streams (e.g., "Design Sync", "QA Testing").
- Pin important guidelines or deadlines at the top of the chat.

### 2. File Organization
- Ensure that important team documents are uploaded to the correct groups.
- Use the **File Manager** to keep track of versions.

### 3. Progress Tracking
- Check in with members via Direct Messages if they haven't updated their status.
- Compile a summary of the team's work for the Team Lead.

## Daily Usage Example
- Check the **Read Receipts** on the morning's task list to ensure everyone has seen it.
- Create a new project group for a cross-functional task.
- Upload the weekly schedule to the **Team Files** section.
