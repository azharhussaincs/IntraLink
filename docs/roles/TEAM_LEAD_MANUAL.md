# Role Manual: Team Lead

## Role Overview
As a **Team Lead**, you are the bridge between upper management (Super User) and the execution teams. You manage personnel and track the progress of your assigned teams.

## Permissions
- Create and Manage Teams.
- Create Users (Team Members, Team Managers, Project Managers).
- Manage Team-wide Groups.
- Submit Daily/Weekly Reports to the Super User.
- Monitor Team Activity and File Transfers.

## Key Workflows

### 1. Building your Team
- Use the **User Management** dashboard to create accounts for your team members.
- Organize them into specific **Teams** or **Departments**.

### 2. Reporting Workflow
- At the end of every day/week, navigate to the **Reporting Module**.
- Fill out the status update form (Tasks completed, blockers, next steps).
- Submit it for Super User review.

### 3. Monitoring
- Use the **Team Dashboard** to see who is online and track the status of shared project files.

## Daily Usage Example
- Start by greeting the team in the **Team Announcement** group.
- Create a new **Project Manager** account for a recently promoted staff member.
- Submit the **Daily Progress Report** before logging off.
