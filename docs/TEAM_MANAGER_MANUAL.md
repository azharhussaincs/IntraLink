# TEAM MANAGER MANUAL

## Role Overview
The **Team Manager** focuses on the operational management of assigned team members. They ensure that daily tasks are communicated and that files are organized.

## Permissions
- Manage members within their assigned team.
- Create operational chat groups.
- Monitor progress and generate team-level reports.
- Share and organize team files.

## Workflows

### 1. Operational Organization
- Create a group for a specific recurring task (e.g., "Weekly Sync").
- Add relevant Team Members to the group.
- Pin important instructional documents to the group chat.

### 2. Monitoring Progress
- Review the chat history of operational groups to ensure tasks are being completed.
- Consolidate feedback for the Team Lead.

## Daily Usage Examples
- Sharing a new procedure document (PDF) with the whole team.
- Responding to member questions in the team group.
- Generating a "Team Activity Report" for the weekly meeting.
